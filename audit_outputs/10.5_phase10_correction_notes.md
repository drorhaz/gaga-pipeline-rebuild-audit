### Clarification A — Step 06 Quaternion Diagnostics: Expanded Logging Scope

**Phase 10 decision:** Q-EXT4a is classified as `LOGGING_ONLY` — "add logging assertion (warn if |q| outside 0.95–1.05 after SavGol); NO hard gate."

**What Phase 11 must add:** The LOGGING_ONLY scope must be expanded. The purpose is not just a single threshold warning — it is to produce enough diagnostic evidence to decide later whether SO(3)-aware smoothing is needed. Phase 11 must include the following logging requirements for S06 in the final target skeleton:

| Diagnostic | Location | What to log |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-renormalization quaternion norm deviation | S06 input | Per-joint: mean, max, p99 of |∥q∥ − 1| before any renormalization |
| Post-renormalization norm status | After SavGol smoothing | Per-joint: confirm max norm deviation after smoothing |
| Renormalization burden per joint | Per-session summary JSON | Per-joint count of frames where |∥q∥ − 1| > 0.05 (i.e., frames needing renormalization) |
| Hemisphere/sign continuity checks | After quat_log computation | Per-joint: count of hemisphere flips detected and corrected |
| SavGol pre-smoothing impact | After SavGol, before quat_log | Per-joint: max norm deviation introduced by SavGol smoothing of quaternion components |

**What must NOT change:**
- `quat_log` remains the default angular velocity method — do not change.
- Do not switch `omega_method` to `5pt` as default — the function `finite_difference_5point()` is a weighted smoother, not a true 5-point stencil (confirmed Phase 4).
- Do not add a hard gate on quaternion norm that could stop processing on clean sessions.
- Do not implement SO(3)-aware smoothing — this is a future v2 candidate pending the diagnostic evidence.

**What this enables:** After Ticket 011 (or bundled into S06 additions in Ticket 007), the renormalization burden logs will reveal whether any joints show systematically high correction burden. This is the prerequisite evidence for deciding whether SO(3)-aware smoothing (e.g., geodesic SavGol) is justified in a future release.
